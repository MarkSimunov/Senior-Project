WIDENER UNIVERSITY INVITES HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS (GRADES 9-12) TO PARTICIPATE IN A TWO AND A HALF HOUR PROGRAMMING CONTEST. THE WIDENER UNIVERSITY PROGRAMMING CONTEST IS AN ANNUAL TRADITION WHERE HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS DISPLAY THEIR PROGRAMMING SKILLS AND PROBLEM SOLVING ABILITIES. TEAMS OF TWO OR THREE STUDENTS COMPETE TO ACHEIVE THE HIGHEST SCORE POSSIBLE BASED UPON RANDOMLY GENERATED SOFTWARE QUESTIONS. TIME AND EFFICIENCY ARE TWO CRITICAL FACTORS IN DETERMINING YOUR SCORE SO PLEASE KEEP THIS IN MIND THORUGHOUT THE CONTEST. BEST OF LUCK TO ALL PARTICIPATING TEAMS!
