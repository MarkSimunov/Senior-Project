Widener's programming competition is an annual tradition where high school students compete to achieve the highest score possible based upon randomly generated software/programming questions. Students assemble teams and have the ability to answer the questions as a group. Answering the questions as quickly and efficiently as possible will be factored into the scoring process. This is important to keep in mind as the competition begins so you may pace your group to stay on appropriate time intervals. Best of luck to all participating teams!
