The Fifteenth (15th) Annual High School Programming Contest







Friday, April 2nd, 2021


Widener University invites high school students (grades 9-12) to participate in a two and a half hour programming contest. The Widener University programming contest is an annual tradition where high school students diplay their programming skills and problem solving abilities. Teams of two or three students compete to achieve the highest score possible based upon randomly generated software questions. Time and efficiency are two critical factors in determing your final score so please keep this in mind throughout the contest. Best of luck to all participating teams!


Prizes:

First Prize = 300 Dollars + Trophy

Second Prize = 200 Dollars + Trophy
Third Prize = 100 Dollars + Trophy


2020 Contest Winners:

First Prize: A team from Garnet Valley High School, PA

Second Prize: A team from Chichester High School, PA
Third Prize: A team from Sun Valley High School, PA
